There are different programming languages that involve web development.

HTML:

The role of html in a website is to provide the structure and content of a webpage , including text , images , videos , and other multimedia elements.

CSS:

CSS is used to add design elements like colors , font styles , and spacing.

JavaScript:

JavaScript is used to add interactivity to the webpages.
